About Me

What I Do

I develop interactive and immersive web experiences targeting some of today's most advanced interfacing technology like augmented reality and virtual reality.

My Vision

To put it simply: I want to push the web into the 3rd dimension. In my experience, the vast majority of today's websites are clinging to yesterday's interfacing capabilities. Basically, humans experience the world in 3D, but we're currently forced to spend our lives interacting with most any digital technology through a 2D interface. I want to create a future in which users can interact with the web in the same way they would interact with anything else in their surrounding physical environment.

Caption Mask

The COVID-19 pandemic has made most everyone's life more difficult. This is especially true for the Deaf and Hard of Hearing Community due to the fact that many deaf people can't read lips while the person they are communicating with is wearing a mask that obscures their mouth. Caption Mask solves this problem by converting the user's speech to text which is displayed on a screen attached to the mask.

Sketch Safe

A carrying case for designers and engineers. It holds papers and design tools in a shock and water resistant housing. The transparent white-board affixed to the front of the case serves as a clipboard, whiteboard, or window to view an existing drawing through and markup with overlay drawings.

See "Industrial Design Projects" for additional details.

Pole Vault Pro

This multitool is a pole vaulter's best friend. Its main function is to clamp onto the end of a pole vault pole, allowing the user to lift and replace the bar that vaulters attempt jumping over. It also doubles as a spike-tightening tool. While not in use, it can be clamped around the roll of grip tap that any serious pole vaulter will have on-hand.

See "Industrial Design Projects" for additional details.

Eco Deo

Designed for the Cradle to Cradle design competition, this fully-recyclable deodorant stick concept focuses on eliminating waste. The cup and lid can easily be separated for cleaning, which makes them recyclable. The hemisherical shape means it ships flat, and uses less packaging to contain a given volume.
